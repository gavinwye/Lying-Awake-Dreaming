Gavin Wye Designer [Product, Interaction, Service, User Experience, Systems...]

I've had many job titles over the last 18 years. I prefer just to use the word Designer, with the caveat that it's about do the right thing at the right time.

Separating design into specific sub-disciplines like Product, Interaction, Service, Content and even User research (which is a design discipline) is helpful when working as part of a team. However, I find, in general those words confuse people who are not designers.
I've even seen designers be confused.

Even when working as part of a team. Where it is nessasary, for whatever reason to distinguish between these different sub-disaplines the most important thing is always to work collabrativley togeter.
